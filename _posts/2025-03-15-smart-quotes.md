## Summary

9 of the 26 sites examined render smart quotes.

For those 9 sites with smart quotes, the source files use:

- smart: 1
- straight: 6
- unknown: 2

## Conclusion

I think rendering smart quotes looks nicer and using straight quotes for the source files is best.
